01-January-2020 
Eight Chinese nationals were quarantined at a hotel in Johor Bahruon 24 January-2020 after coming into contact with an infected personin neighbouring Singapore.91193Despite early reports of them testing negative for the virus,91293three of them were confirmed to be infected on 25 January and subsequently quarantined at the Sungai Buloh Hospitalin Selangor.9139391493The Malaysian health ministrypublished guidelines on the virus and established designated hospitals in each of Malaysias statesto manage any positive cases.91593The Malaysian public were reminded by local authorities to take precautionary measures in the wake of the virus threat with those travelling to China have been advised to stay away from animal farms and markets in the country and to not eat raw or semicooked meats.91693Following several earlier suspected cases in Sabahs capital of Kota Kinabalu, all direct flights between the state with China were stopped indefinitely.9179391893On 24 January-2020, a twoyearold child who was suspected to have been infected was detained along with their parents.
